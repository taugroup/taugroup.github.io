# Research

Our goal is to build techniques and develop Applications that enable us to address the challenges we find most interesting. 

Our Research areas include:

- MOR, SciML, 3D Modeling, VR/AR
- Geospatial Analysis, Scientific Visualization, Signal Processing
- Natural Language Processing
- Machine Learning
- Deep Learning
- Data Curation, Digital Twin, IoT
